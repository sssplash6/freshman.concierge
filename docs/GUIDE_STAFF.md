# 🤖 Concierge Bot — Staff Guide

Your assistant for class reminders, consult links, and quick check-ins.
Everything is shown in **your** timezone.

---

## 🚀 Getting started

1. Open the bot and tap **`/start`**.
2. Enter your timezone (e.g. `GMT+5`, `+3`, or `Asia/Tashkent`) and confirm.

That's it — you're registered. Re-run `/start` anytime to update your timezone.

---

## 🎛 Your menu buttons

| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| 📅 **My Schedule** | Shows your next 5 upcoming sessions. |
| 🔗 **Set Link** | Save your consultation booking link for a cohort. It's auto-posted to that cohort's group chat. |
| 🌍 **Timezone** | Change the timezone all your times are shown in. |

---

## 🔔 What the bot sends you automatically

| Nudge | When | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **Session reminder** | 1 hour before each lecture (consults: 10:00 AM on the day) | Just a heads-up — no action needed. |
| **Completion check** ✅❌ | 2 hours after a session | Tap **Yes** or **No**. If **No**, type a short reason. |
| **Saturday nudge** 🗓 | Saturday 5:00 PM | Set **next week's** consult link, or tap **Done** on a weekly task. |
| **Task check-in** 📌 | ~2 h before a task deadline, then at the deadline | Tap **Yes**/**No** at the deadline (**No** → short reason). |

---

## 💡 Tips

- Set your timezone once — every time you see is then **local to you**.
- For check-ins, **No** always asks for a one-line reason — keep it short.
- Tap **/cancel** to back out of any step (note: a bare `/cancel` outside a flow unregisters you).
- Lost? Tap **/menu** to bring the buttons back.
