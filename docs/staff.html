🤖 Staff Guide

Concierge Bot

Your assistant for class reminders, consult links, and quick check-ins. Every time is shown in your timezone.

🚀

Getting started

  1. Open the bot and tap /start.
  2. Enter your timezone (e.g. GMT+5, +3, or Asia/Tashkent) and confirm.
  3. You're registered — re-run /start anytime to change your timezone.
🎛

Your menu buttons

  • 📅 My ScheduleYour next 5 upcoming sessions.
  • 🔗 Set LinkSave your consult booking link for a cohort — it's auto-posted to that cohort's group chat.
  • 🌍 TimezoneChange the timezone all your times are shown in.
🔔

What the bot sends you

  • Session reminder1h beforeHeads-up before each lecture (consults: 10:00 AM on the day). No action needed.
  • Completion check ✅❌2h afterTap Yes or No. If No, type a short reason.
  • Saturday nudge 🗓Sat 5 PMSet next week's consult link, or tap Done on a weekly task.
  • Task check-in 📌at deadlineIf Sega assigns you a task: a heads-up ~2h before, then Yes/No.
✨

Tips

  • Set your timezone once — every time then shows local to you.
  • "No" always asks for a one-line reason — keep it short.
  • Lost? Tap /menu to bring the buttons back.
