# 🎓 Concierge Bot — TA Guide

Your assistant for homework checks and reminders.
Everything is shown in **your** timezone.

---

## 🚀 Getting started

1. Open the bot and tap **`/start`**.
2. Enter your timezone (e.g. `GMT+5`, `+3`, or `Asia/Tashkent`) and confirm.

You're registered. Re-run `/start` anytime to update your timezone.

---

## 📚 Your main job: Homework checks

For each lecture/seminar in **your assigned cohort**, the bot messages you:

> **3 days after the session** → *"Did you check homework for this session?"*

- Tap **✅ Yes** when it's done.
- Tap **❌ No**, then type a short reason.

Your response rate (✅ vs ❌) is tracked automatically — no extra steps.

---

## 🎛 Your menu buttons

| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| 📅 **My Schedule** | Shows your next upcoming sessions. |
| 🔗 **Set Link** | Save a booking link for a cohort (if you run consults). |
| 🌍 **Timezone** | Change the timezone all your times are shown in. |

---

## 🔔 Other nudges you may get

| Nudge | When | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **Task check-in** 📌 | If Sega assigns you a task | Tap **Yes**/**No** at the deadline (**No** → short reason). |
| **Completion check** ✅❌ | 2 h after a session you run | Tap **Yes**/**No** (**No** → reason). |

---

## 💡 Tips

- Set your timezone once — every time you see is then **local to you**.
- Homework checks arrive **3 days after** the session, so check on time.
- **No** always asks for a one-line reason — keep it short.
- Lost? Tap **/menu** to bring the buttons back.
