🤖 CONCIERGE BOT — STAFF GUIDE

Your assistant for class reminders, consult links, and quick check-ins.
All times are shown in YOUR timezone.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🚀 GETTING STARTED
1) Tap /start
2) Enter your timezone (e.g. GMT+5, +3, or Asia/Tashkent) and confirm.
Done — you're registered. Re-run /start anytime to change your timezone.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🎛 YOUR MENU BUTTONS
📅 My Schedule — your next 5 upcoming sessions.
🔗 Set Link — save your consult booking link for a cohort (auto-posts to its group chat).
🌍 Timezone — change the timezone your times are shown in.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🔔 WHAT THE BOT SENDS YOU
• Session reminder — 1 hour before each lecture (consults: 10:00 AM on the day). Just a heads-up.
• Completion check ✅❌ — 2 hours after a session. Tap Yes or No (No → type a short reason).
• Saturday nudge 🗓 — Saturdays at 5:00 PM. Set NEXT week's consult link, or tap Done on a weekly task.
• Task check-in 📌 — if Sega assigns you a task: a heads-up ~2h before, then Yes/No at the deadline.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💡 TIPS
• Set your timezone once — every time then shows local to you.
• "No" always asks for a one-line reason — keep it short.
• Lost? Tap /menu to bring the buttons back.
