🎓 CONCIERGE BOT — TA GUIDE

Your assistant for homework checks and reminders.
All times are shown in YOUR timezone.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🚀 GETTING STARTED
1) Tap /start
2) Enter your timezone (e.g. GMT+5, +3, or Asia/Tashkent) and confirm.
Done — you're registered. Re-run /start anytime to change your timezone.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📚 YOUR MAIN JOB: HOMEWORK CHECKS
For each lecture/seminar in your assigned cohort, 3 days after the session
the bot asks: "Did you check homework for this session?"
• Tap ✅ Yes when it's done.
• Tap ❌ No, then type a short reason.
Your response rate (✅ vs ❌) is tracked automatically.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🎛 YOUR MENU BUTTONS
📅 My Schedule — your next upcoming sessions.
🔗 Set Link — save a booking link for a cohort (if you run consults).
🌍 Timezone — change the timezone your times are shown in.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🔔 OTHER NUDGES YOU MAY GET
• Task check-in 📌 — if Sega assigns you a task: Yes/No at the deadline (No → short reason).
• Completion check ✅❌ — 2h after a session you run: Yes/No (No → reason).

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💡 TIPS
• Set your timezone once — every time then shows local to you.
• Homework checks arrive 3 days after the session — check on time.
• "No" always asks for a one-line reason — keep it short.
• Lost? Tap /menu to bring the buttons back.
